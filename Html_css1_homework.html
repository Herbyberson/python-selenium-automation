How to Make Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients:

  • any size of frozen dough
  • ½ (12 ounce) can Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Instruction:

  1. Get It Ready. open the dough and unwrapped it and pat it out and put the cheese and pepperoni on the dough.
  2. Put It in the Oven. set the oven on 475 degrees put the pizza in the oven and wait 10 to 15 min.
  3. Enjoy It.
